A New Beginning

An Exciting New Job

I started working for the Halifax Regional Municipality back in 2016 as an apprentice electrician. I loved worked with the public and was so excited to begin a new career, it seemed like everything I wanted it to be until...

The Realization

I was working outside in the cold of a maritime winter, standing on a ladder during a blizzard changing a light outside of an abandoned building. I got back into my work van and checked my phone and saw a picture of my brother working ona website in front of a roaring fireplace.

The Change

I promptly returned home from work, with a desire for change urging me onwards. As soon as my fingers defrosted I was on the NSCC website applying for the IT Programming course. I had one year left of my apprenticeship which would give me plenty of time to figure things out, or so I thought...

The Pandemic

2020 was about to throw a wrench into my plans. I went into work only to find out my department was getting cut. I was laid off and a bit dejected upon returning home. The world was scary and still trying to come to terms with what was happening. I was beginning to wonder where I would work next, when the best news arrived to my house!

The News

I could hardly contain my excitement gripping the acceptance letter in my hand. This was exactly the change I hoped for. I had been testing out some development methods with my brother as a guide and I just knew this was the best path for me. I could barely wait until September hit.

The Challenge

I had a blast learning to code for two years. It was so fun, creative, and rewarding. I loved how the code pieces fit together like puzzles. Even when it got hard, I was always into it. And guess what? After school finished, I aced a couple of interviews and got my first job!

The Last Two Years

I have spent the last two years working at Resulta doing web development. It has been such an amazing time, and I have learned so much and met so many people. Unfortunately do to restructing I was caught up in the most recent layoffs. While it was sad news that I would no longer be working with such an amazing group of people I am veryexcited for my next adventure to start. I hope you had fun learning a little bit about me and my journey to get me here!
